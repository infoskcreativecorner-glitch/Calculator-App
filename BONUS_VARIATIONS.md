## B. UI Theme Variations (Using Your Brand Colors)

### 1. Neon Glass Theme
- Background: `#24272F` to `#2F333C` gradient
- Accent: `#5EE9E6`
- Buttons: glassmorphism with soft glow
- Ideal for: modern presentation and premium look

### 2. Corporate Minimal Theme
- Background: solid `#1F2229`
- Accent: muted cyan `#49C9C6`
- Flat buttons with thin outlines
- Ideal for: formal academic submissions

### 3. Dark Dashboard Theme
- Background: `#20242B` with subtle texture
- Accent: `#37D6D2`
- Numeric keys dark, operator keys highlighted
- Includes compact top info bar
- Ideal for: practical and tech-oriented demo
